Cricket Game, Week2_pythonproject

A cricket game is for 2 player.
Each player will participate in toss
Winner of toss can decide who will bat
Batsman will play 12 balls in which he will not be out and random score will be generated for each player in range (0, 6)
After 1st inning player 2 has to proceed for 2nd inning
Same way he will score runs and chase player 1
After comparision of scores Winner will be concluded
